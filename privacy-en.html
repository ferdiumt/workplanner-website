Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Thank you for using WorkPlanner. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains how our application handles data.

Data Collection

WorkPlanner does not collect any personal data. The app does not:

  • Send any data to remote servers over the internet
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share any information with third parties
  • Require account creation or sign-in

Data Storage

All tasks, projects and settings you create are stored locally on your device only. Data is located at:

  • ~/Library/Application Support/WorkPlanner/workplanner.db

This data is accessible only by you. When you delete the app, your data is deleted with it.

Automatic Backups

WorkPlanner creates a local backup file when the app closes. These backups are stored in:

  • ~/Library/Application Support/WorkPlanner/backups/

These backups are entirely local and are never transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

WorkPlanner does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will publish the update on this page. For significant changes, we may notify you within the app.
